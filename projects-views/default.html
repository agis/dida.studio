2.2.1 Almyros Resort, New Built, 2022

Almyros Resort is a 5star luxury resort hotel in Northern Corfu, Greece, near the Natura area of the famous sand dunes.
The proposal suggests a 75 room hotel with 13 separate room buildings, 3 common buildings, 2 common swimming pools, reaching nearly 7000sqm of built area.
The architectural language follows simple geometries with details of traditional Corfu elements such as the famous arches, the line of columns.
Together with the nature of olive trees/ beach pine trees and the special softscape of the sand dunes, forms a dense, yet original built environment.
