Christina Tsirangelou develops a work language embracing modernity with a vernacular vocabulary, yet developing a strength thought environmentally sustainable conscious methods and materials. Working from her studio in Athens (Greece) she aims to create works that intervent with both social and technical possibilities.

In 2009, she founded, along with her architect partner, 157+173 designers, an extrovert Product Design independent studio, which produced locally and traded worldwide, especially to Japan, USA and Northern Europe, with notable mentions through the press/radio/tv, gaining recognition though trade/design fairs as a Brand. In 2021-2023 she became Associate Creative director/Partner of the USP multidisciplinary team, one of the biggest architectural firms in Greece, gaining a precious know-how of large scale New-Built projects in the Hotel, Retail & Residence sector. The big growth of architects, from 53 to 100+ in 2023, challenged her abilities to create co-operative methods of directing and leading the projects. Academically, during 2021, she served as an assistant teacher in Aristotles University of Thessaloniki in Greece, in the BA of Interior Architectural Design.

During her university years she joined the practice of Arch. Prof. In UTH, Giorgos Triantafilidis, as an assistant teacher the BA Interior Architecture in AKTO+Middlesex Univestity, by the side of Arch. Prof. D. Tsasis, and as an assistant & employee in Sparch Architects office by Arch. Prof. Rena Sakelaridou & Arch.Assistant Morpho Papanikolaou, gaining the very valuable experience of architecture next to the masters.
